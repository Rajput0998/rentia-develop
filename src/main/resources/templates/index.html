Frequently asked questions?

Let's Start a Free of Questions And Get a Quick Support That Will Help You to Know Us


On our platform, you can easily search for coliving spaces in Gurugram by specifying location, budget, and preferred amenities. Our search feature will display matching results.

Common amenities in coliving spaces include furnished rooms, high-speed internet, communal kitchens, laundry facilities, cleaning services, social spaces, and sometimes gyms or co-working areas.

Booking a coliving space is simple on our platform. After finding a suitable listing, you can directly book it by following the provided instructions, which typically involve filling out a booking form and making a secure payment.

Yes, most coliving spaces include utilities such as electricity, water, and internet in the monthly rent. However, it's essential to check the specific details provided in the listing.

Coliving spaces offer a variety of room types, including private rooms, shared rooms, and sometimes studio apartments. Listings on our platform will indicate the available room types for each property.

In most cases, coliving spaces come fully furnished, and personalizing the room may have limitations. However, it's advisable to check the specific policies mentioned in the listing or contact the coliving brand for more information.

Each listing on our platform includes contact details for the coliving brand or property manager. You can reach out to them directly via phone or email to ask any additional questions or clarify doubts.

Our cancellation and refund policies may vary depending on the coliving brand and property. It's recommended to review the cancellation policy mentioned on the listing or contact the coliving brand directly for detailed information.

We value your feedback! After your stay in a coliving space booked through our platform, you will have the opportunity to leave a review and provide feedback. Look for the review section on the property's page to share your experience.
