About us

Rentia is a platform that aims to make co-living accessible and convenient for everyone. The platform connects tenants with the best co-living brands in Gurugram, providing them with detailed information about different brands and their properties. This makes it easier for tenants to find a room that fits their budget and needs.

  • Mission

    Rentia's mission is to make co-living accessible and convenient for everyone by being the go-to platform for booking, promoting top brands, and driving the growth of the co-living industry.

  • Vision

    Rentia Aims to Establish Itself as the Foremost Platform for Co-living Solutions in India, Offering Affordable, Top-notch Accommodation Options with Uncompromising Quality and Exemplary Customer Satisfaction.
